\section{Other Things to Pay Attention to}
\label{sec:oth_imp}

\begin{itemize}
\item
{\bf For FYP A the page limit is strictly 4 pages,
while for FYP B the page limit is strictly 10 pages. Appendices are
\underline{not} allowed.}
\item
Make sure to write the nouns and verbs in the project title, Department names,
and section and subsection headings with an uppercase first letter.
\item
An equation can be written like this:
\begin{equation}
q_i = \displaystyle \frac{K_i}{c_i} e^{-c_i z_i} \textup{tan} ( \beta_i)
\label{eq:q_i}
\end{equation}
$q_i$ is the baseflow per unit width (m$^2$s$^{-1}$),
$K_i$ the hydraulic conductivity (ms$^{-1}$),
$c_i$ the exponential decay coefficient (m$^{-1}$),
$z_i$ the depth to the water table (m),
and $\beta_i$ the slope of the land surface.
As is shown for Equation \ref{eq:q_i},
always make sure to explain all the variables in your equations,
and also to add the units or dimensions.
\item
It is recommended to write in the passive as much as possible.
Thus, write ``The measurements were taken with a temporal interval of
15 seconds" instead of ``we took the measurements with a temporal
interval of 15 seconds".
\item
In the tab ``Getting Started" on moodle there is a large
amount of information on how to perform a literature search.
\end{itemize}
